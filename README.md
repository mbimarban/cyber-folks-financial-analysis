---

## Overview

This project presents a **financial analysis of Cyber_Folks Group** in a form of an interactive CFO board pack.

The goal is to combine:
- **clear storytelling (HTML)**
- **data & model (Excel)**
- **decision-oriented insights**

---

## Key areas covered

- Revenue growth and scalability of the business model  
- EBITDA margin and operational efficiency  
- Free Cash Flow and capital intensity  
- Net Debt / EBITDA and financial leverage  
- Impact of M&A on performance  

---

## Key takeaway

Cyber_Folks demonstrates strong growth and profitability, but the key question remains:

> Can EBITDA from acquisitions grow faster than financing and integration costs?
